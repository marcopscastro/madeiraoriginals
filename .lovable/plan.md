

# Madeira Originals — Storefront Landing Page

## Design System Setup
- Custom Tailwind colors: Canvas (#F9F7F2), Atlantic Blue (#1B365D), Pico Red (#A41C2E), Canary Yellow (#F4B942)
- Google Fonts: Montserrat (headings, bold/uppercase/tracked) + Inter (body)
- Flat, minimalist aesthetic — no shadows, no rounded CTA buttons, no glossy effects

## Assets
- Copy the uploaded product images (tee, hat, tote bag) and logo into the project for use in the hero, product grid, and header

## Landing Page Sections (in order)

### 1. Sticky Header
- Logo text "MADEIRA ORIGINALS" in Pico Red (Montserrat Bold) on the left
- Nav links center: Shop Tees | Canvas Goods | Headwear | Our Story
- Right: Lucide Search, User, ShoppingCart icons in Atlantic Blue
- Canvas background

### 2. Hero Section
- Left: large headline "Madeiran heritage. Zero stiffness." + subheadline + sharp "Shop the Originals" CTA button (Pico Red, no border-radius)
- Right: lifestyle image (uploaded photo of person wearing tee)

### 3. Value Proposition Banner
- Full-width, 3 columns: 📍 Designed in Madeira | 🧵 Premium Heavyweight Cotton | 🚫 0% Tourist Trap

### 4. Bestsellers Product Grid
